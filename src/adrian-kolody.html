Mr. Kolody is a cryptocurrency entrepreneur and leader who has helped to develop the framework of the industry since 2011. Mr. Kolody is a recognized expert in cybersecurity, blockchain architecture & engineering development, smart contracts integration, and capital markets derivatives exchanges, with implementation experience in leading all aspects of the proprietary software applications development, innovation and monetization in the cryptocurrency space. Mr. Kolody has also provided both custodial and non-custodial application solutions ranging from tokenized securities and early stage Ethereum decentralized applications (dapps). Mr. Kolody is also a featured speaker at numerous crypto, blockchain and fintech securitization initiatives in Chicago, including at the Illinois Institute of Technology, Founders (University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign) and Shirley Ryan Ability Lab, a research hospital where clinicians, scientists, innovators and technologists apply real- time research to physical medicine and rehabilitation. Mr. Kolody is also the founder of Domination Finance Exchange, a non-custodial custom derivatives financial instruments exchange that enables traders to speculate on Domination Pairs and generates APY (annual percentage yield) on stablecoins for liquidity providers.
