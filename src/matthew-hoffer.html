Matt Hoffer recently joined Cushman & Wakefield as a Director in the Tenant Advisory Group. Matt has more than 17 years of experience advising clients in the strategic planning of their real estate as well as in the implementation of resulting assignments, including tenant representation, site selection, sublease/disposition and strategic consulting assignments.

Prior to joining his last firm, JLL, in 2013, Matt worked as an independent broker, primarily advising Corinthian Colleges, a client he had serviced in Chicago since joining JLL’s predecessor firm, The Staubach Company, in 1999. Matt provided a single point of contact while establishing Corinthian’s presence in Chicago with six new campus locations.
